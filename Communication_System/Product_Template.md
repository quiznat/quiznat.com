# Product Voice (Generic)

**Primary Surface**: Game site, itch.io page  
**Purpose**: Create a safe, inviting fantasy for players  
**Audience**: Players (often kids + parents), not builders  
**Time Horizon**: The experience, not the version

---

## Core Characteristics

- Warm
- Reassuring
- Emotion-first
- Narrative
- Explicitly non-technical

---

## What This Voice Does

- Frames features as feelings
- Emphasizes safety, fun, and delight
- Encourages play and confidence
- Celebrates progress and rewards

---

## What This Voice Avoids

- Version numbers (unless required)
- Dev tools or AI mentions
- Build timelines
- Technical bugs (unless reframed gently)

---

---

## Generic Product Update Template (Family-Friendly Game)

1. **Friendly greeting to players**
2. **Short emotional summary of the update**  
   (what feels better, more fun, or more exciting)
3. **Section: What's New**  
   Each feature framed as an experience, not a mechanic
4. **Section: Fixes & Polish**  
   Reframed as "smoother," "cleaner," "more reliable"
5. **Section: Why This Matters**  
   Tie changes back to confidence, joy, or ease
6. **Warm close with invitation to play**
