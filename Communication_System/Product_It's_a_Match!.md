# Product Voice (Specific): It's a Match!

This is a specialization of the Product Voice, tuned for:

- Children
- Parents
- Classroom / family environments

---

## It's a Match! Voice Personality

**Archetype**: Cheerful guide / storybook narrator  
**Emotional Goal**: Confidence, pride, delight  
**World Tone**: Safe, colorful, celebratory

---

## Language Rules

- Use exclamation sparingly but naturally
- Favor verbs tied to play: flip, match, build, collect
- Avoid pressure words: fast, competitive, intense
- Always assume a first-time player might be reading
